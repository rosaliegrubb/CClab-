    What we know so far about Doodle Bop?

    The local Leprechaun hunters of Ireland were on a special mission. It was the first rainbow of summer and they thought they were bound to find some Leprechauns. Instead, they discovered a new creature. The new creature was then taken to scientist Rosalie Grubb's laboratory and research center to be researched. The creature, Doodle Bop, comes from the Doodlae family who all have one eye and are considered the happiest creatures in the world. Doodle Bop was discovered to be the happiest of them all. Doodle Bop is a friendly creature that adds color to anything it touches.

    Guidelines for human interaction with Doodle Bop

      Using your mouse, drag Doodle Bop over any of the squares to change the color to black. Using your mouse again, drag Doodle Bop over any of the squares to change them back to their original colors. Drag Doodle Bop to the blue background to make rainbow iterations of the character. Press the space bar to reset the sketch.
