Key Characteristics

  • A happy creature that makes everything happy.
  • Makes rainbows in the sky.
  • Has the ability to float in space and move on the ground.

Habitat

  • The blue portion is space where gravity does not exist.
  • The grid portion is the ground where gravity exists.

What do we know so far about Doodle Bop?

Doodle Bop is a friendly creature who adds color to anything it touches.
